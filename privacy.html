Privacy Policy

The Privacy Policy applies to the personal data provided by users and clients of Zelux Spark, both for the provision of various information services and for other interactions, usually in electronic format, as well as for client participation in the company's training programs and promotions, in the process of providing technical support and service.

Zelux Spark reserves the right to make changes and additions to the current provisions of the Privacy Policy, and these changes and additions will take precedence over the current provisions. Please note that the services provided may contain links to third-party resources that have their own Privacy Policy provisions. In such cases, Zelux Spark is not responsible for the content and compliance with the Privacy Policy provisions of any third-party company. By providing your personal data, the user gives full consent to its processing by the methods provided for by the current provisions of the project's Privacy Policy.

Collection, Storage, and Disposal of Personal Data

Zelux Spark collects personal data only with the user's consent and solely to provide access to the platform's services that require private information: access to closed blog materials, bot development consultations, participation in promotions, and technical support. To prevent personal data leakage, a full range of information security measures (technical and organizational) is used.

The maximum storage period for a client's personal data is 75 years from the date the company receives the private information. In other cases, the company stores the client's personal data until the activities of the zelux-spark.blog project cease.

The destruction, depersonalization, or blocking of personal data or parts thereof is carried out to exclude the possibility of further processing of this information.

Zelux Spark collects the following categories of user personal data:

Technical Information:

  • access time and IP address;
  • sources of referral to the company's online resource;
  • web pages visited by the user;
  • views of advertising and informational banners;
  • other technical information provided by the user's browser;
  • phone number (in case of filling out feedback forms or calling the numbers indicated on the site).

Upon registration or filling out forms on the site, the company collects personal information necessary for user authentication, personalization of services, and feedback:

  • user name;
  • e-mail and phone number.

Information on User Actions:

  • information on agreements and contracts between the client and the project;
  • information on requests sent to support;
  • data on services and educational materials used by the client;
  • payments made and other financial information required by applicable law.

Purposes of Processing Clients' Personal Data:

  • providing access to AI tools and platform services;
  • taking customer preferences into account when developing new products or updating educational content;
  • informing the client about promotions, discounts, and new AI assistant capabilities via email;
  • providing expert feedback.

Zelux Spark does not provide clients' personal data to third parties, except in the following cases:

  • obtaining direct user consent to transfer their data to a third party;
  • at the request of law enforcement agencies in accordance with current legislation;
  • merger or acquisition of the company.

Use of Cookies and Pixel Tags

Cookies allow for the collection of information about IP addresses, operating systems, browser types, and referral URLs. This is necessary to improve the services provided (saving preferences, traffic analysis, etc.). Pixel tags are used for the graphical display of anonymized information (visitor counters). If you wish, you can disable Cookies in your browser settings at any time.

User Rights Regarding Personal Data

If a user expresses a desire to stop processing their personal data or change it, they should contact us by email: support@zelux-spark.blog.
