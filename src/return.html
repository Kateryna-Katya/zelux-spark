Conditions for a Refund

You may be eligible for a full or partial refund in the following situations:

  • Content Mismatch: If the content of the provided course or educational module on bot development significantly differs from the program stated on the zelux-spark.blog website at the time of purchase.
  • Technical Issues on Our Side: In the event of critical technical problems on the Zelux Spark platform that make the learning process or access to AI tools impossible and were not resolved by our technical support within a reasonable timeframe.
  • Cooling-off Period: If you decide to cancel your purchase within 14 (fourteen) calendar days of payment, provided that you have not yet accessed a significant volume of the educational materials (see the "Exceptions" section).

Refund Request Procedure

To initiate a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Draft an official request and send it to our support email: support@zelux-spark.blog.
  2. Ensure the subject line reads: "Refund Request".
  3. In the body of the email, provide full details: your name, the email address used during registration, and the exact name of the paid course or service.
  4. Describe in detail the reason for your refund request, referring to one of the points in the "Conditions for a Refund" section.
  5. Upon receipt, our team will review the request within 3-5 business days and contact you.
